Mandatory gear

The gear you leave with.

The thousand-milers publish a mandatory gear list — no team starts without it. Ours has three instruments, one for each dimension of the work ahead. They're yours after the hour, to run on your own enterprise.

Dimension 01 · Your data estate

Data Source Mapping

A way to chart every source that should fuel your agents — ranked by priority and readiness, with the seams where sources pull together and the role each one plays on the sled.

Dimension 02 · Your workflows

Workflow Mapping

A model for enumerating, locating, and validating the places agents can step in — owner, data required, what success looks like, and a difficulty × impact ranking with estimated ROI.

Dimension 03 · The human–agent interface

Interface Mapping

A blueprint for rationalizing every point where agents meet your people — Slack, email, meetings, and beyond — and shaping the handoffs, escalations, and guardrails so adoption sticks.
